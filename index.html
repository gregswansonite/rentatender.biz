About the Bartenders

Our staff has been hand picked to offer you the best for your party. We pride ourselves in high quality and prompt drink service no matter the occasion or location.

All of our bartenders are trained in alcohol awareness and are TABC certified. However, the host of the event is ultimately responsible for all invited guests and their actions.

RentaTender.biz reserves the right to refuse service to any guest(s) who seem to have had too much to drink, per our judgment. In addition, RentaTender.biz reserves the right to ID anyone who appears to be under 35 years of age.
