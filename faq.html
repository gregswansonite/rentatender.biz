How will your bartenders be dressed for my event?

Bartenders will show up dressed in black polo shirts and jeans. If any other attire or costume is appropriate please let us know.

Do you provide blenders?

We do not provide any appliances. If you choose to have frozen/blended drinks we ask that you provide the blender as well as, an appropriate area near the bar with running water.

Are you licensed?

Yes, bartenders are professionally trained and TABC certified.

Will you supply the alcohol?

We do not buy or sell alcohol. We can help with consultation on what to buy for your party if needed.

Will you provide the glassware?

We do not provide glassware.

Can you provide servers to assist with food service?

Yes, we can provide servers that can help with beverage or food service.

What does the customer need to provide?

The customer may provide the alcohol, mixers, ice and cups. If desired, RentaTender.biz can provide all-inclusive service upon request.

A flat table will be required for our bartenders to properly prepare and serve cocktails. We have portable tables for rent at your request. If kegs are being served at your event, you will have to provide the taps for the kegs.

All items being consumed by your guests are your responsiblity. This includes alcohol, mixers, ice, fuit garnish, table ware (including cups), and straws that you wish to serve your guests
