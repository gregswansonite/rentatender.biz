Drink Services

Our bartenders, can and will create, the finest of cocktails. Our list of Suggested Mixers for Full Bar Service will allow creation of the finest cocktails around.

Suggested Mixers for Full Bar Service consist of:
  • Mixers: Lime Juice, Grenadine, Sour Mix, Bloody Mary mix
  • Fruit: Limes, Lemons, Olives, Cherries
  • Sodas: Coke, Diet Coke, Tonic, Sprite, Ginger Ale, Club Soda, Water
  • Juices: Orange, Cranberry, Pineapple
  • Consumables: Stirrers, Beverage Napkins, Fruit Picks, Cups, Ice

Add a Non-Alchoholic Beverage Station

Adding a self serve non-alcoholic beverage station for your guests is a creative option that they will love. Our infusion jars allow you to be as decadent as you like with your beverage options. Select any three infused beverages from our available choices.

  • Iced Tea
  • Peach Infused Iced Tea
  • Lemonade
  • Strawberry Infused Lemonade
  • Iced Water
  • Citrus Infused Ice Water
  • Cucumber Water with Mint and Lemon
  • Strawberry Basil Lemon Water
  • Watermelon Mint Lemon Water
  • Pineapple Orange Water
Your beverage station will come complete with 3 infusion jars and all ingredients needed. Your bartender will set up your station and replenish them throughout your event. Price is based on number of guests.

If you are having any specialty drinks or if additional product is needed our bartenders will require that you provide them with an ingredients menu to insure that the cocktails are made to your specifications.
